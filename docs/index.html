A Little Bit About Me

Growing up in Los Angeles, California was a large reason why I ventured out of state for my higher education. It was time for me to experience a new culture and pace of life. I entered Oklahoma State University as a Civil Engineer major, but after one semester and a coding class, I quickly discovered my passion for programming. With very little hesitance, I changed my major to Computer Science and began my journey as a developer. If you would like to know more about me, see my About Me page.
